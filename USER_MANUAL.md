# GEOSUREPATH - Client User Manual

Welcome to GEOSUREPATH, the premium GPS Tracking and Fleet Management solution. This guide walks you through the core features of your portal.

## 1. Login & Registration

**Accessing Your Account**
Navigate to the main portal URL provided by your administrator. 
- If you are a new client, click **Register**. Upon successful registration, your verified credentials will be dispatched instantly to your registered WhatsApp number for absolute security.
- Enter your Email and Password on the Login screen. You will be redirected to the Client Dashboard.

## 2. Navigating the Client Dashboard

The GEOSUREPATH Client Dashboard is split into three main actionable areas:

### A. The Sidebar Navigation (Left)
- **Objects**: Click here to view a real-time list of all vehicles assigned to your account. You can see if they are moving (green dot), idling (yellow dot), or offline. Click on any vehicle to instantly pan the map to its live location.
- **Events**: A live ticker of important systemic notifications, such as SOS button presses, Geofence Entry/Exit, or continuous overspeeding.
- **Places (Geofencing)**: Manage your customizable geographical borders here. You can click on the map to draw Polygons or Circular zones. When your vehicles enter or exit these zones, you will be notified.
- **History**: Use the date and time picker to load historical routes. Click **Play** to watch the vehicle retrace its path at your desired speed.

### B. The Live Map Window (Center)
The Leaflet-powered map continuously streams your fleet's coordinates. 
- Vehicle markers (cars with teardrop pins) physically rotate to reflect their current driving heading.
- You can freely drag and collapse the **Floating Tool Panels**:
  - **Zone Rules Config**
  - **Alert Modules**
  - **Live Telemetry Grid** (Expands to show deep hardware sensor data like voltage, fuel levels, and current speed).

### C. The Module Tabs (Top)
Navigate to advanced modules via the top navigation bar:
- **Alerts**: Extensive configuration of which events (Overspeed, Idling, Power Cut) trigger Push or SMS notifications.
- **Fuel Logs**: An area to manually record fuel refills or visualize hardware fuel-rod telemetry to prevent fuel theft.
- **Maintenance**: Schedule and track part lifespans. Add parts (e.g., Brake Pads, Oil) and set a kilometer limit. The system tracks mileage automatically and warns you when a part needs replacement.
- **Billing**: View your active subscription, top up your account, and access your referral code to unlock free months.

## 3. Advanced Feature: Remote Engine Blocking

For the highest degree of security, GEOSUREPATH includes a Remote Immobilization feature. 

**How to trigger an Engine Cut:**
1. Open the **Objects** tab on the left sidebar.
2. Locate the vehicle you wish to immobilize. Click the **Edit/Configure** sub-menu for that vehicle.
3. Toggle the **Engine Block** switch to **ON**.
4. **Security Confirmation**: A high-security OTP dialog will appear on-screen. You must enter the 6-digit code shown to verify your intent.
5. Once confirmed, the command is securely queued precisely for your vehicle's hardware. Depending on connectivity, the engine will safely cut off.
6. To restore ignition, toggle the switch back to **OFF** and confirm again.
